About Us

We frame spaces that become legacies. Our commitment goes beyond lumber and fasteners—we engineer dependable structures, streamline schedules, and protect the trust our clients place in us.

Our Story

Founded by field-tested supervisors who saw the gap between speed and craftsmanship, Build Bliss Construction was created to prove you can have both. What started as a small, disciplined framing crew has grown into a collaborative unit supporting multi-family, custom residential, and commercial projects across the Lower Mainland and Interior.

Every project we touch is guided by a simple principle: deliver structural excellence with zero drama. That means proactive communication, precise layout, clean sites, and crews that show up prepared—every single day.

We invest heavily in training, tooling, and workflow optimization. Laser layout, safety-first sequencing, and transparent reporting help GCs and owners keep confidence high while schedules stay protected.
